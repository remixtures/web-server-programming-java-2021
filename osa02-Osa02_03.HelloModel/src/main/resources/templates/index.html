The story

Once upon a time, there was a person called person, who liked to play. As person grew older, a small box was uncovered from the basement of an old abandoned house. The box had the text Commodore 64 written on it -- person found the box very interesting, but did not know what to do with it.

To unravel the mystery, person sought out the owners of the house, and asked them about the box. They told that it is a sacred relic from ancient times, when people could conjure things with their hands.

"Why have I not heard about this before", asked person. "The things that the owners of such relics have conjured are all around us, but we do not see them anymore as the people from ancient times used to see them", answered one of the owners. "They are as much a part of us, as we are a part of them, hidden in our midst", continued the other.

How can something be hidden, but still be there, person could not understand. And so, the journey began.
